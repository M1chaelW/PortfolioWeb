Policies & Procedures

Welcome to CyberTech. We are committed to providing reliable and secure web hosting services. Below, you will find our detailed policies and procedures, ensuring a transparent and smooth experience for our clients.

Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

Preamble

At CyberTech, our Acceptable Use Policy is designed to ensure a secure, ethical, and respectful use of our web hosting services. By using our services, clients agree to comply with this policy.

Definition

For the purpose of this policy:

  • "Services" refer to any web hosting, support, or related resources provided by CyberTech.
  • "User" refers to clients, their representatives, or anyone accessing the services.

Policy Statement

This policy applies to all clients and users of CyberTech’s web hosting services. It outlines acceptable and prohibited activities to maintain service integrity and security.

Acceptable Uses

  • Hosting lawful websites, content, or services.
  • Using CyberTech services for personal or business purposes within the bounds of the law.
  • Complying with all local, national, and international regulations.

Unacceptable Uses

  • Hosting or sharing illegal, harmful, or fraudulent content (e.g., malware, phishing sites).
  • Spamming, hacking, or attempting unauthorized access to any system or network.
  • Activities violating intellectual property rights, privacy laws, or any applicable regulations.

Violations/Sanctions

Violations may result in account suspension, termination, or legal action. To report violations, contact cybertechmedhat@gmail.com
